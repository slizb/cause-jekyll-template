Beyond the mountains, there are mountains.

As the Haitian proverb alludes, life’s struggles never stop coming. Unfortunately for the poorest of Haiti, one more burden can often be the difference between life and death. Our mission is to equip those in most need with the tools to make it over life’s next mountain.
